Progression Tracking

Watch your metrics evolve over time across all your training sessions.

Avg Balls Cleared per Rack

Your primary performance metric. Combines position, potting, and decision making.

Misses per rack

True misses end a ball run when the outcome is pot miss (or legacy both). No shot (position) and playable are soft logs for streaks here; no-shot is still counted in its own KPI.

Best run (streaks)

Longest ball run without a true miss (same rules as session reports). No-shot / playable logs do not reset the streak.

Flow efficiency

Balls cleared ÷ (balls cleared + training misses) per session — continuity, not raw success only.

True miss rate

True misses ÷ ended racks per session (hard failures per rack). Lower is stricter run discipline.

Rack conversion rate

Ended racks where you cleared all 9 balls ÷ total ended racks that session — raw run-out rate.

Rack consistency

Balls cleared per rack: worst vs average vs best. A wide gap flags volatility.

Recovery vs failed recovery

After each training miss: % where the next log was not a true miss (recovery) vs was a true miss (failed). Denominator = training misses in that session (see docs/recovery-metric.md).

"No Shot Due to Position" Count

Critical metric. We expect a sharp drop early as positional play improves.

Miss type (true misses only)

Share of tag selections on run-breaking events only—aligned with pot-miss style failures, not every training log.

True miss by ball

Ball number on true misses only (streak-breaking). Shape-only logs are excluded.
