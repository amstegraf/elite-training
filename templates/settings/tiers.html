There is no extra percentage row: once every KPI is in the Semi-pro band (at or above the Semi-pro minima), you are already at the top KPI band. Elite vs Semi-pro on the dashboard is decided by tier points and the Tier point cuts below (same composite formula, higher points → Elite).

Calculation Rules

Score weights

POS / CONV / POT weights must sum to 1.00 (within 0.01). Imbalance penalty reduces inflated scores when one KPI is much higher than the others.

Tier points scale

Adjusted composite (0-4) is multiplied by this value for tier points (dashboard, sandbox). KPI scores are interpolated continuously between your percentage thresholds, then imbalance penalty is applied before scaling. Elite spans from the last cut up to 4 × scale (e.g. 4000 when scale is 1000).
